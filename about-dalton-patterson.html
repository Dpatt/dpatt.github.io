Dalton Patterson | Digital Strategist

Hi, I'm Dalton Patterson

For the last seven years, I've been on a journey discovering what it takes to become a Digital Strategist. This adventure has led me to learn a lot about digital, but even more about who I am as a professional.

Humble Beginnings

There has always been something about art, and design that has appealed to me. As far back I as can remember, I've loved colors, fonts, and shapes. I would fill notebooks with doodles of cars, action-heroes, and draw my favorite brand's logos. I guess you could say, I was alway meant to be in a creative field.

My passion for design continued to grow as I grew, and pretty soon I learned the art of the hustle. I was no longer doodling in sketchbooks just because I enjoyed it. I was making money. In my early 20's, I opened a digital print and web business.

A Real-world Education

Owning my own business in my 20's, wasn't what I thought it would be. At the time, I felt there was so much more for me to learn to become a successful entrepreneur. I enrolled in Full Sail Univeristy to learn as much as I could about the web. In 2013, I was awarded a Bachelor of Science in Web Design and Development with honors.

After a short break, I re-enrolled and obtained a Graduate Certificate in Internet Marketing. The skills I've learned have given my many opportunities, but what I am most grateful for is the confidence my education has given me.

Into the Real-World

After college, I was offered an excellent opportunity to move my family to Colorado Spring, CO. and work with a global non-profit as their Front-End Designer. Since 2015, I have continued to work at Focus designing and developing custom digital products using modern web frameworks.

Investing in myself is still a high priority for me. I have taught myself methodologies such as Content Strategy, Responsive Web Design, and Digital Strategy within the last two years.
